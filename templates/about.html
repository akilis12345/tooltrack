About ToolTrack

A smarter way to manage barangay equipment and community resources.

What is ToolTrack?

ToolTrack is a barangay management system designed to organize, monitor, and track community-owned equipment and resources. It helps barangay officials efficiently handle borrowing requests, maintain accurate records, and ensure accountability.

Purpose

The system replaces manual logs and paperwork with a digital solution, making it easier to track borrowed items, due dates, and responsible users.

Key Features

Equipment Tracking

Monitor all barangay tools in one place.

Borrow Requests

Simple and fast request system.

History Logs

Track all borrowing activities.

Who Can Use It?

ToolTrack is designed for barangay officials and authorized users managing community equipment.
